# AI Reputation Judge Architecture

## Flow

User
↓
Submit Contribution
↓
GenLayer Intelligent Contract
↓
AI Evaluation
↓
Consensus Validation
↓
Reputation Score
↓
On-Chain Storage

---

## Evaluation Criteria

The AI evaluates:

- Contribution Quality
- Community Impact
- Originality
- Evidence Credibility

---

## Outputs

- Reputation Score
- Evaluation Reason
- Evaluation Count

---

## Why This Requires GenLayer

Traditional smart contracts cannot reason about subjective information.

GenLayer enables decentralized evaluation through AI-powered consensus mechanisms.

This allows the contract to analyze contribution quality and generate reputation scores based on contextual information.
